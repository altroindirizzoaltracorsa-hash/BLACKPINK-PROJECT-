// How To Stream

Stream
Smart

Streaming smart is just as important as streaming often. Follow these steps to maximize the impact of every play.

Organized, consistent streaming moves the needle — not just for charts, but for algorithm visibility and long-term legacy.


Open Spotify →
01

Free or Premium — Both Count

Premium and free accounts both count equally toward stream numbers. Premium is preferred for stronger algorithm signals, but every stream matters.

02

Disable Autoplay & No Looping

Turn off Autoplay in the Spotify mobile app settings. Do not loop — let the playlist run naturally. Our daily playlist is shared on @BPSpotifySchool.

03

Stream on Spotify

Use the daily playlist provided by @BPSpotifySchool. Keep volume above 30%, headphones connected, and never mute.

04

Add to Your Library

Save the songs to Liked Songs and personal playlists. This signals strong engagement to the algorithm.

05

Share & Rally Blinks

Tag fellow Blinks, post stream screenshots, keep the energy up. Community momentum is our biggest advantage.
