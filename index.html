// How To Stream

Stream
Smart

Streaming smart is just as important as streaming often. Follow these steps to maximize the impact of every play.

Organized, consistent streaming moves the needle — not just for charts, but for algorithm visibility and long-term legacy.


Open Spotify →
01

Use a Premium Account

Premium streams count at full weight. Free tier still counts, but premium is preferred for algorithm signals.

02

Stream on a Playlist

Create a playlist with the target songs. Set volume above 30%, keep headphones connected, let it loop. No muting.

03

Watch YouTube MVs Fully

Views must be at least 50% of the video to fully count. Watch all the way through when you can.

04

Add to Your Library

Save songs to Liked Songs and personal playlists. This signals strong engagement to the algorithm.

05

Share & Rally Blinks

Tag fellow Blinks, post stream screenshots, keep the energy up. Community momentum is our biggest advantage.
