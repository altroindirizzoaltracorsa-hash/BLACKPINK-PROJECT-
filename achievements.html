◆ Guinness World Records ICON · 2026 ◆

In 2026, BLACKPINK became the first K-pop group — and first Asian act — to receive the Guinness World Records ICON designation. Reserved for artists whose achievements have permanently reshaped their field, the ICON status recognises a decade of firsts spanning music, charts, streaming, and live performance. Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa hold this honour jointly as BLACKPINK.
