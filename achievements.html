◆ Guinness World Records ICON ◆

In 2026, BLACKPINK became the first K-pop group to receive the Guinness World Records ICON designation — an honour reserved for artists whose record-breaking achievements have permanently reshaped their industry. Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa hold this status jointly as a group, in addition to their individual titles.
